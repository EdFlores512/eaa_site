THE WONDERS OF THE EDWARDS AQUIFER

Creating an Unforgettable Experience

The Education Outreach Center will offer a new bold and exciting departure from the traditional classroom outreach previously offered by the EAA. Positioned on the critical Edwards Aquifer Recharge Zone and nearby EAA Field Research Observatory Park, The EOC will have unique access to a world-class cadre of scientists, technicians, and real time aquifer data.

"A family-friendly environment where adults and children can interact and learn."

"Every encounter between a child and an exhibit is an eye-opening experience."

"Sometimes the best way to learn is to get your hands dirty - literally."
